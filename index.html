Our Story & Philosophy

Education of Tomorrow, Rooted in Tradition

Abundant Grace Preparatory Montessori School is your world of excellent students and future leaders, located in the heart of Benin City, Edo State.

We follow the authentic Montessori method — an approach that respects each child's unique developmental timeline. Our carefully prepared environments invite children to explore, question, and master skills at their own pace, supported by quality teachers and necessary materials to aid your child's learning.
